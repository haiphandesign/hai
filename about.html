This is the online portfolio of Hai Phan.
Hai Phan is a Saigon based graphic designer and design master's student who embraces details in his works and believes in layout, typography and strong imagery. Hai values imperfections around him and feels compelled to rise to its challenge in order to improve processes, reimagine experiences and transform the relationship between brands and people.
Originate from HCMC, VN — Hai Phan got his Bachelor of Design at the Royal Melbourne Institure of Technology (RMIT University) in Australia where he return a few year after to continue his study in Master of Design at Monash University.
Hai is currently working as the founder and executive art director for Hyperion™; co-owner and art director for Deadend™ and co-founder of the text-based role-playing platform Dẫn Truyện .
Hai (obviously) doesn't have all the answer,
just the tireless curiosity to pursue them.
